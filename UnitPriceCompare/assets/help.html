Unit Price Compare

Contact author: leoliang@gmail.com

Feature

Calculate the unit price of similar products sold in different sizes/qualities and tell which item is less expensive.

Supports comparing between different unit (e.g. kg vs lb).

Supports quantity input by arithmetic expression (e.g. 250*6+100).
